This Web site contains complete sets of high-resolution archival images of manuscripts from the collection of the University of Pennsylvania Library and other contributing institutions, along with machine-readable TEI P5 descriptions and technical metadata. All materials on this site are in the public domain or released under Creative Commons licenses as Free Cultural Works. Please see specific collections and documents for applicable license terms.
